Cafe au Lait

Definition du cafe

A café au lait is a classic French coffee drink made with equal parts brewed coffee and steamed milk. The name translates to "coffee with milk" in French. It's a creamy, smooth beverage, typically served in a bowl or a wide cup. How to Make Café au Lait: 1. Brew Coffee: Use strong brewed coffee or a French press for an authentic flavor. 2. Heat Milk: Steam or heat milk on the stovetop until warm, then froth it lightly (optional, as it usually doesn’t require much foam like a latte). 3. Combine: Pour equal parts coffee and steamed milk into a cup or bowl. 4. Optional: Sweeten to taste or add a touch of cinnamon or cocoa powder for flavor.
